"home"

"home" is an ongoing photographic study of the places the homeless often occupy in New York City. These call into question the role of architecture to invite or deter unplanned-for activities/occupations. For most of us, the concept of "home" (as the foil of "house/apartment/etc.") is isolated from its host environment; this exercise begins to question whether the study of "homelessness" as the extended occupation of bodies in public space can yield a more humane urban environment for the less fortunate.
